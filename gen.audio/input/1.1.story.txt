[male_narrator] It has long been my privilege, dear listener, to accompany Sherlock Holmes through a series of adventures which have often placed us in the most curious and perplexing situations. To me, however, it remains first and foremost a story of Holmes’s brilliance and of my own humble apprenticeship in the science of observation.
[male_narrator] The events began on a bleak October morning. [male_stsimon] She had removed it after the ceremony.

[male_holmes] Your honor, my lord, must stand upon the truth, not upon illusion.

[male_holmes] You shall hear from me within twenty-four hours.

[male_watson] You mean—Mr. Francis Moulton?
[male_holmes] Precisely. We must discover his whereabouts.
